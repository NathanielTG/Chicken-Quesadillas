Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Sliced chicken
  • Salt & pepper
  • Taco seasoning
  • Flour
  • Chicken stock
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tortillas
  • Cheddar cheese (or another flavor of your choosing)
  • Scallions
  • Sour cream and salsa on the side (OPTIONAL)

Instructions

    1. Pre-heat oven to 200 F
    2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
    3. Add salt & pepper to chicken
    4. Cook chicken until no longer pink (roughly 8 minutes)
    5. Add taco seasoning to chicken
    6. Add chicken stock and simmer for roughly 8 minutes
    7. Remove chicken from skillet into bowl and pour sauce into a second, separate bowl
    8. In another skillet, heat a teaspoon of vegetable oil and add tortilla when heated
    9. Add cheese and scallions, then add some chicken to the tortilla
    10. Add some sauce from the second bown onto the tortilla
    11. Add some more cheese and top with another tortilla layer
    12. Carefully flip tortilla
    13. Cook until brown on both sides
    14. Voila!
